gegeben

$d = 0,00038m$
$n = 2$
$x_2 = 0,141m$

gesucht:
$\lambda$

Lösung:
$sin(\alpha_n) = n* {\lambda \over d}$
	| *d | / n

${d*sin(\alpha_2) \over n} = \lambda$

$\tan(\alpha) \approx \sin(\alpha)$

${d*\tan(\alpha_2) \over n} = \lambda$
$\tan(\alpha_2) = {x_2 \over s_0}$
${d*x_2 \over n*s_0} = \lambda = {0,00038m * 0,141m \over 2*8,272m}$


$$\lambda = (d*x_2)/n*s_0$$
